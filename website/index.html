Welcome to our official platform

The DABOKPA Technical Institute started as a Girl’s Boarding Middle School and later evolved into a Vocational/ Technical Institute in 1980. It is located along the Tamale-Yendi/Salaga trunk road, about 3km from the town center in the Tamale Metropolis An educational institution of academic excellence where well-motivated and highly qualified staff use modern technology and teaching methods in a very clean environment to inculcate good morals and self-confidence in students.

To continuously enhance the training of middle and top-level Technical personnel for the Public and Private sector industries as well as Non-Governmental Organizations, both in Ghana and beyond.
